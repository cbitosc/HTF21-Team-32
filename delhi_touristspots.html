Humayun Tomb

4.5/5(5,024 reviews)
The Nizammudin railway station in Delhi is the nearest railway station to Humayun's Tomb. If you take the Metro, then Jorbagh or Race Course stations (both on the Yellow line) are the nearest.
Timings:All days of the week 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Visit duration:2 hours

Entry Fee:

Rs 30 per person for Indians
Rs 500 per person for Foreigners

Qutub Minar

4.4/5 (4,824 reviews)
Local buses are available from various points in Delhi. Local guided tours are also conducted by Delhi Tourism and private operators. The nearest metro station is Qutub Minar station on yellow line. You can hire an auto from the metro station to reach here.
Visit duration: 3 hours,Timings:All days of the week 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Entry Fee:

Rs 30 per person for Indians
Rs 500 per person for Foreigners

Red Fort

4.1/5 (2,117 reviews)
Red Fort, one of the most prominent landmarks of Delhi, can easily be accessed by roadways. Located at Netaji Subhash Marg, the nearest metro station is Chandni Chowk. From there you can either opt for an auto or a cycle rickshaw
Visit duration:3 hours ,Timings:except monday 9:30am-4:30pm

Entry Fee:

Rs 30 for child
Rs 60 for adults
Rs 500 for Foreigners
